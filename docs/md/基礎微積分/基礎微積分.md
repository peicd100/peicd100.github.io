## 綜合除法
[來源](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=28zj2-L9Zhw&t=6s)

\[
(a_3x^3 + a_2x^2 + a_1x + a_0) = (b_2x^2 + b_1x + b_0)(x - a) + r        
\]

\[
(a_3x^3 + a_2x^2 + a_1x + a_0) \div (x - a)=(b_2x^2 + b_1x + b_0)...r
\]

### 綜合除法過程：

\[
\begin{array}{r|rrrr}
a & a_3 & a_2 & a_1 & a_0 \\
  &     & b_2a & b_1a & b_0a \\
\hline
  & b_2 & b_1 & b_0 & r \\
\end{array}
\]


### 範例

欲計算

\[
(2x^3 - 3x^2 - 4x + 5) \div  (x - 2)     
\]

綜合除法

\[
\begin{array}{r|rrrr}
2 & 2 & -3 & -4 & 5 \\
  &   & 4  &  2 & -4 \\
\hline
  & 2 &  1 & -2 & 1 \\
\end{array}
\]


結果：

\( 商 = 2x^2 + x - 2 \)  
\( 餘數 = 1 \)
